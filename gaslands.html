Gaslands

Kitbashed toy cars for post-apocalyptic vehicular combat.

Official Gaslands Site

Gaslands is a tabletop system focused around the use of kitbashed Hot Wheels/Matchbox style toy cars, making it a very accessible and affordable way to get a lot of the satisfaction of "painting miniatures" without spending hundreds or thousands of dollars on "official models".
