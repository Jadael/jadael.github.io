Old West Poker Cards

Buy on Game Crafter

A weathered, vintage-style poker deck inspired by 19th century American playing cards. The face cards feature period-appropriate illustrations in the classic European court card style, while every card has an aged, distressed texture.

The deck includes standard 52 cards plus jokers and reference cards for poker hand rankings and popular game variants like Texas Hold'em, Omaha, and Five Card Draw.

Originally designed in 2011.

Playscii Cards

Buy on Game Crafter Playscii by JP LeBreton

A pixel-art playing card deck created entirely in Playscii, JP LeBreton's ASCII/ANSI art program. Every element is built from text characters, giving the cards a distinctly retro-digital aesthetic.

The face cards feature original character designs rendered in chunky pixels, while the number cards use stylized pip arrangements. The back design features colorful pixel creatures.

Created in 2024.
